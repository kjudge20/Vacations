Disney World

Disney World is the most magical place on Earth. Disney World, located in Orlando, Florida, includes waterparks, amusement rides, and beautiful places to stay.

Hotels/ Resorts to stay at:

  • Disney's Saratoga Springs Resort&Spa

    • Disney's Saratoga Springs Resort&Spa includes relaxation by the pool, full deluxe rooms with kitchens and living rooms for you to stay in, and a destressing spa.

  • Disney's Old Key West Resort

    • Disney's Old Key West Resort includes 1 and 2 bedroom studios, big pools (indoor and outdoor), and many restuarants with accents of the old times in Key West.

  • Disney's Caribbean Beach Resort

    • Disney's Caribbean Beach Resort includes fun by the waterpark-like pool, pirate themed rooms, and multiple restaurants that will fit your appetite.

Things to do & places to go

  • Travel through the four parts of Disney World: Magic Kingdom, Animal Kingdom, Hollywood Studios, and Epcot
  • Enjoy food from various countries through Epcot Resort
  • Enjoy the weather at the two waterparks within Disney World: Typhoon Lagoon and Blizzard Beach

Restaurants

  • Have breakfast, lunch, or dinner with the princesses in Cinderella's castle
  • Enjoy some Italian food at Tomorrowland Terrace Restaurant
  • Try the Coral Reef Restaurant in Epcot for some seafood dinner
