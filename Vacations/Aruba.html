Aruba

Aruba is a beautiful place to travel to that includes amazing sceneries.

Hotels to stay at:

  • Aruba Beach Villas

    • Stay at the Aruba Beach Villas and overlook the Caribbean Coast

  • Tropicana Aruba Resort & Casino

    • Stay at the Tropicana Aruba Resort & Casino and be right next to the beach

Things to do & places to go:

  • Travel to Plam Island, Aruba to sail and take boat rides along the coast
  • Go to Eagle Beach to snorkle and relax on the beach

Restaurants:

  • Matthew's Beachside Restaurant

    • traditional breakfast, lunch, and dinner

  • Barefoot Restaurant

    • seafood
